Why Fauvism Matters

Fauvism is an art movement that emphasizes bold, unnatural color, emotional expression, and simplified forms. Rather than copying reality, Fauvist artists used color to convey feeling and intensity.

Core Characteristics of Fauvism

  1. Color Expression
    • Bright, exaggerated colors
    • Emotion over realism
  2. Brushwork
    • Loose and visible strokes
    • Energetic application
  3. Simplified Form
    • Reduced detail
    • Focus on overall composition
