Why Fauvism Matters

Fauvism emphasizes bold color, emotional expression, and simplified forms rather than realistic representation. It prioritizes feeling over accuracy.

Core Characteristics of Fauvism

  1. Color Expression
    • Intense, unnatural hues
    • Color used emotionally
  2. Brushwork
    • Loose strokes
    • Visible texture
  3. Simplified Form
    • Reduced detail
    • Focus on composition
