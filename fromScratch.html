Why Fauvism Matters

Fauvism emphasizes bold color, emotional expression, and simplified forms rather than realistic representation. It prioritizes feeling over accuracy, reshaping how color is used in modern art.

Core Characteristics of Fauvism

  1. Color Expression
    • Intense, unnatural hues
    • Color used emotionally rather than realistically
  2. Brushwork
    • Loose and spontaneous strokes
    • Visible painterly texture
  3. Simplified Form
    • Reduced detail
    • Focus on overall composition
