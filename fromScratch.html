Why Fauvism Matters

Fauvism emphasizes bold color, emotional expression, and simplified forms rather than realism. Artists used color freely to express feeling rather than accuracy.

Core Characteristics of Fauvism

  1. Color Expression
    • Bright, exaggerated hues
    • Emotion over realism
  2. Brushwork
    • Loose and visible strokes
    • Energetic application
  3. Simplified Form
    • Reduced detail
    • Focus on composition
